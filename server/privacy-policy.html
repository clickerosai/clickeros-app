1. Introduction

Clickeros AI ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Clickeros AI mobile application (the "App") available on Android and iOS. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By downloading and using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please do not use the App.

This policy was last updated on May 11, 2026 and is effective immediately for new users and within 30 days for existing users.

2. App Permissions & Data Use

The Clickeros AI app requests the following device permissions. Each permission is used only for the purpose stated below and is never activated without your direct action. You may revoke any permission at any time through your device settings.

📷
CAMERA
android.permission.CAMERA
Purpose Capture photos and videos for ad creatives, upload product images for AI ad generation, and scan QR codes for account linking or platform integrations.
Data Collected Only images and videos you explicitly capture and choose to upload. The camera is never accessed in the background.
Shared With Images are processed by our AI servers to generate ad copy. They are never sold or shared with third parties for advertising.
🎙️
RECORD_AUDIO
android.permission.RECORD_AUDIO
Purpose Enable voice-to-text input for the AI Strategy Copilot, allowing you to dictate campaign briefs and product descriptions. Also used for AI video script narration.
Data Collected Audio is captured only when you actively initiate a voice session. It is processed in real-time for transcription and not stored permanently after processing.
Shared With Audio is sent to our secure AI transcription service for real-time processing only. Raw audio is not retained or shared with third parties.
📱
READ_PHONE_STATE
android.permission.READ_PHONE_STATE
Purpose Detect active calls to pause audio playback and notifications during phone calls. Also used for device identification to maintain secure login sessions.
Data Collected Call state (idle, ringing, or in-call) and a non-reversible device identifier for session security. We do not access your phone number, IMEI, or call logs.
Shared With Used internally for session management only. Not shared with third parties, advertisers, or data brokers.
👤
GET_ACCOUNTS
android.permission.GET_ACCOUNTS
Purpose Facilitate single sign-on (SSO) with Google and other account providers, allowing quick login without a separate password. Pre-fills your email in account forms.
Data Collected Only the email address and display name of the account you explicitly select and authorize. We do not read passwords or access account data beyond what you consent to share.
Shared With Used for authentication only. Your email is stored in our secure database to identify your account. It is not sold or shared for marketing purposes.

  • 🗄️ AES-256 at Rest
    All stored data is encrypted with AES-256.
  • 🔍 Security Audits
    Regular penetration testing and security reviews.

While we strive to protect your personal data, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but commit to notifying you of any breach within 72 hours as required by applicable law.
