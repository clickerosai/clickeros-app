1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit our website or use our web-based features. They help us recognize your device, remember your preferences, and understand how you interact with our platform. Similar technologies include local storage, session storage, pixels, and SDK identifiers used in our mobile app.

This Cookie Policy applies to the Clickeros AI website at clickeros.ai, the web version of our app, and any web-based features embedded in our mobile application. It should be read alongside our Privacy Policy.

4. Mobile App Tracking Technologies

Our mobile application does not use traditional browser cookies. Instead, we use the following equivalent technologies:

TechnologyPurposeStorage Location
AsyncStorage / SecureStoreStore your login session, preferences, and app state locally on your device.Device local storage
Device Identifier (non-reversible)A hashed, non-reversible identifier used for session security and fraud prevention. Not linked to your identity.Secure device keychain
Push Notification TokenA token generated by Expo/FCM to deliver push notifications. Stored on our servers only while notifications are enabled.Our servers + device

You can clear all locally stored app data by uninstalling the Clickeros AI app or clearing app data in your device settings.

5. Your Choices and Controls

Browser Cookies: You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling strictly necessary cookies will prevent you from using core features of the platform.

Mobile App: You can opt out of analytics tracking by contacting us at privacy@clickeros.ai. You can disable push notifications at any time through your device notification settings.

Do Not Track: We respect browser DNT signals. When DNT is enabled, we disable all non-essential analytics tracking for your session.

EU/UK Users (GDPR/UK GDPR): You have the right to withdraw consent for non-essential cookies at any time. Strictly necessary cookies do not require consent as they are essential for the service to function.
